On the basis of our background and our discussions with Dr Colombo, I believe our 
involvement in the INDAGTE proposal could be in the following clusters/workpackages:

a) Cluster 3 "MA - e-Factory Application"

WP2 "Agentification of Automation Functions"

On the basis of our expertise, Paragon could develop/improve the special monitoring 
software for the diagnosis of the status of machinery and the scheduling of its 
preventive maintaining. It is well known that an unscheduled stop in the production 
process involves high costs. Furthermore, in the case of machinery break- downs the 
response of the technical support is crucial, since the down-time is proportional to 
the aforementioned costs.

Paragon will develop an Artificial Neural Network (ANN) for the prognosis of the 
status of the machinery based on their behaviour, as well as other data (usually of 
statistical nature, like time from last break-down/service, etc). On the basis of 
these data the ANN will be trained to "foresee" the type of fault and the possible 
timing. On the basis of this, it will be able to notify, well in advance, the 
service department for the necessary spare parts and schedule a service in the 
proper time, minimizing the related costs.


b) Cluster 5 "MA - e-Factory Enterprise Integration"

WP2 "Integration with Operational Business Procedures"

Paragon will develop a special optimization system, based on genetic Algorithms, the 
operation optimization of the whole- e- Factory (site-level). On the basis of the 
operation schedule/procedures (managerial level) and the status/simulations of the 
factory floor (developed in Cluster 2), the optimization system will be able to 
fine-tune the operation of the e-Factory.

Of course, when we receive more information about the proposal and during its 
development, we will be able to provide more information concerning our involvement 
and align our contribution with the other partners.
