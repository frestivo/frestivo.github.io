Work area

The aim is to develop computing methods for Systems Service assurance. This means 
to detect and control systems failures, degradation or disturbances. Apart from 
systems availability, service concept includes security and environmental concerns.

The objective is to develop methods that allow an intelligent service assurance by 
including autonomous decision-making algorithms with learning capabilities.

Activities

1. System service assurance by:
a) Monitoring: data gathering and failures, degradation or disturbances detection.
b) Decision making: diagnosis, evaluation, prognosis.

2. Learning, in order to automatically improve service assurance performance.

Knowledge areas:
Signal processing, Diagnosis algorithms, Predictive Maintenance, Reliability.

Technologies: 
Data mining, Neural networks, Fuzzy Logic, Knowledge Management, Case-based 
reasoning.
