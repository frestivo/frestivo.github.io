Personal Data

  • Born in Porto, Portugal, in 1947 January 11.

Academic Studies

  • Licensee in Electrical Engineering by the School of Engineering of the University of Porto, with the final classification of 17 out of 20 (1970).
  • Doctor of Philosophy by the University of Sussex, with a thesis entitled Microprocessors in Signal Processing (1981).
  • Título de Agregado by the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering of the School of Engineering of the University of Porto (1998).

Professional Career

Present positions

  • Member of Board of Directors and Scientific Co-ordinator at IDIT - Institute for Development and Innovation in Technology (1999-).
  • Associate Professor at the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering of the School of Engineering of the University of Porto (1988-).

Previous positions

  • Senior Researcher at CCP - CIM Centre of Porto (1990-1999), Member of the Board of Directors (1991-1994), President (1995-1999).
  • Senior Engineer at MultiSistemas, Lda. (1983-1987).
  • Assistant Professor at the Department of Electrical Engineering of the School of Engineering of the University of Porto (1981-1988).
  • Head of the Department of Electrical Engineering of the School of Engineering of the University of Porto (1974-1975).
  • Senior Lecturer at the Department of Electrical Engineering of the School of Engineering of the University of Porto (1973-1981).
  • Researcher at the Centre of Electrical Engineering of the University of Porto (1971-1990).
  • Junior Lecturer at the Department of Electrical Engineering of the School of Engineering of the University of Porto (1971-1973).
